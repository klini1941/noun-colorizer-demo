Noun Colorizer Demo v2

A “predict → reveal → fade” demo. Paste text, predict the anchor nouns, then click Reveal lines to underline likely nouns in dark blue.

Step 1: Before revealing anything, notice which words feel like anchors (people, places, things, ideas).
Dark-blue underline = likely noun
Notes: This is a lightweight demo (no external services). It uses a small built-in noun list + simple heuristics (suffixes like -tion, -ment, -ness). It simulates the experience, not perfect grammar.
